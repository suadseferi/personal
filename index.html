The magic of AI is making it work for people.

Suad Seferi is a leading artificial intelligence strategist and the founder of AI NOW Society, the first association for AI in North Macedonia, with a strong background in cybersecurity and digital trust.

He specializes in AI adoption, digital transformation, cybersecurity, and AI ethics for businesses, public institutions, and communities.

Suad develops AI readiness and cybersecurity assessments, leads workshops, and provides consulting on AI strategy, cyber risk management, and policy implementation.
